Ranger

The project employs strategically placed sensors in forests, equipped with sound recognition technology to detect signs of deforestation like vehicle movements, chainsaws, and tree cutting. Real-time alerts, including precise location details, are transmitted to authorities, enabling swift intervention.The technology integrates GPS for accuracy, reliable communication protocols, cloud-based storage, and analytics for historical trends. With scalability and cost-effectiveness in mind, the project aims to proactively combat deforestation, contributing to the preservation of vital ecosystems and sustainable forest management.
